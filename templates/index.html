About Us

StartupCoaching.in is India’s pioneering platform designed to support startups through their most critical growth phase. We bridge the gap between seed stage and Series A by offering a powerful mix of AI-driven insights, mentorship, and actionable strategies.

Our goal is simple — to help Indian startups thrive. With expert coaches, curated tools, and a vibrant community, we ensure your startup journey is smarter, faster, and more resilient.

Our Vision: To create a robust startup ecosystem where every founder has the guidance and resources to succeed.
